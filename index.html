About Me!

Hello, my name is Joshua Johnson. I feel in love with wanting to learn web design and development when I stumbled upon a video that Brad Hussey made on youtube about creating websites. This fascinated me so much that I started looking into classes from places like Udemy and Treehouse learning. Eventually, I went back to school where I started my path in Computer Programming with the emphasis on web applications. I am currently still enrolled and loving it. In fact this site is one of my final projects for my Web development and Javascript classes.

A little more about me though. I grew up in Georgia, right outside the Atlanta, in a Town called Stone Mountain. From there, I graduated Highschool and didn't know what to do with my life, so I bounced around from job to job till finally a friend of mine convince me to join the Marines. I primary did this so that I could pay for my schooling, but also saw it as an opportunity to get some skill set and do right for my country. I served for Four years in the infantry and from there I went on to college. I ended up with a B.S. in Environmental Science beacuse I wanted to work out doors. 4 years later, I feel out of it and wanted more with my life. I still had some time left on my GI bill and decided to go back for my Computer Science degree, and this is where I am today.

So welcome to my site and lets start a journey together as I learn to code some front end stuff and have fun at the same time.
